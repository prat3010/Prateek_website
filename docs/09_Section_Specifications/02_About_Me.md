# **About Me**

## **Purpose**

The About section transforms curiosity into confidence.

By the time a visitor reaches this section, they already know what I do.

This section answers a different question:

**Who is the person behind the work?**

Rather than listing achievements, the section should communicate values, mindset, and the way I approach building products.

Visitors should leave feeling that they understand not only my skills, but also how I think.

---

# **Product Philosophy**

People hire people before they hire skills.

The purpose of this section is not to convince visitors that I know technology.

The purpose is to convince visitors that I solve problems thoughtfully.

Whether the visitor is a recruiter or a client, they should begin building confidence in working with me.

---

# **Visitor Goals**

After reading this section, visitors should understand:

- how I approach my work
- what motivates me
- how I solve problems
- what kind of builder I am
- why my work feels different

The section should answer emotional questions more than technical ones.

---

# **Content Philosophy**

This section should explain:

Why I build.

How I think.

How I learn.

How I approach new challenges.

Avoid turning this section into a biography.

Visitors care less about chronological history and more about mindset.

---

# **Storytelling**

Rather than saying:

“I know React.”

Communicate:

“I enjoy solving problems by combining thoughtful design with scalable engineering.”

Focus on principles rather than technologies.

Technologies change.

Mindset lasts.

---

# **Human Connection**

Visitors should finish this section feeling that they have met a real person.

The writing should remain:

- authentic
- conversational
- professional
- honest

Avoid sounding robotic.

Avoid sounding overly polished.

Small imperfections often create more trust than excessive marketing language.

---

# **Credibility**

Support statements with evidence whenever possible.

Instead of making claims,

demonstrate them.

Projects.

Experiments.

Portfolio itself.

Documentation.

Workflow.

Execution builds credibility.

---

# **AI Positioning**

Artificial intelligence may be mentioned as part of my workflow.

However:

AI should never become my identity.

The emphasis should remain on:

- problem solving
- product thinking
- engineering judgement
- creativity

Visitors should conclude that AI helps me work faster, not think for me.

---

# **Adaptive Behaviour**

This section should adapt:

- opening paragraph
- emphasis
- examples
- closing statement

The overall story should remain consistent.

Only the perspective changes.

---

# **Relationship With Other Sections**

The About section connects:

Hero

↓

Skills / Services

↓

Projects

It serves as the bridge between first impressions and evidence.

---

# **Accessibility**

Ensure:

- semantic headings
- readable typography
- logical reading order
- keyboard accessibility
- motion preference support

The section should remain equally effective for all users.

---

# **Performance**

This section should remain lightweight.

Avoid unnecessary visual effects.

The focus should remain on the content.

---

# **Future Expansion**

Possible future additions:

- personal timeline
- journey highlights
- values
- working principles
- behind-the-scenes content
- interactive milestones

Future additions should deepen understanding rather than increase complexity.

---

# **Definition of Success**

The About section succeeds when visitors think:

“I understand how this person thinks.”

rather than

“I know which technologies this person uses.”

Understanding creates trust.

Trust creates opportunity.

---

# **Acceptance Criteria**

- The section explains mindset rather than biography.
- Communication adapts naturally for each audience.
- Azure and Noir influence personality without changing facts.
- AI is positioned as a workflow enhancement rather than an identity.
- Content remains authentic and evidence-based.
- Every editable element is CMS-managed.
- Accessibility requirements are satisfied.
- Performance remains lightweight.
- Visitors feel a stronger personal connection after reading the section.
